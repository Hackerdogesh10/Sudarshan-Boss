Our Models

Model M1

The BMW M1, a mid-engine sports car, is a striking wedge-shaped design by Giorgetto Giugiaro and ItalDesign. It's known for its low height and aerodynamic efficiency, allowing for a low center of gravity and optimal cornering speeds. The M1 was one of the fastest sports cars of its time, with a top speed of 262 km/h and acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h in 5.6 seconds

Model M2

The BMW M2 Competition is a high-performance coupe known for its agility, precision, and powerful engine. It features a twin-turbocharged inline-6 cylinder engine, revised chassis tuning, and upgraded brake systems for enhanced performance. The M2 Competition is a compact, sporty coupe that delivers a thrilling driving experience.

Model M3

The BMW M3 Competition is a high-performance version of the BMW 3 Series, developed by BMW's motorsport division, M GmbH. It's known for its powerful engine, aggressive styling, and advanced driving dynamics. The M3 Competition features a 6-cylinder M TwinPower Turbo engine, offering exceptional power and a distinctive M sound. The car also boasts features like adaptive M suspension, M-specific pedals, and optional lightweight M Carbon bucket seats.

Model M5

The BMW M5 is a high-performance sedan known for its powerful engine, sporty handling, and luxurious comfort. It's a blend of business sedan and sports car, offering a compelling mix of comfort and performance. The current generation, the G90, features a plug-in hybrid system with a V8 engine and electric motor, resulting in a system power of 727 hp.

Model M8

The BMW M8 is a high-performance luxury sports car offered in Coupe, Gran Coupe, and Convertible configurations. It features a 4.4-liter V8 engine with a twin-turbocharged system, producing a significant amount of power, reaching up to 625 hp in some models. The M8's performance is enhanced by advanced technologies like BMW M Mode and Adaptive Msuspension.
