BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) is a German luxury automotive manufacturer known for its sporty, high-performance vehicles and innovative technologies. BMW's design is characterized by a long hood, short overhangs, and a recessed greenhouse, contributing to a dynamic silhouette. They offer a wide range of models, including sedans, SUVs, coupes, and sports cars, as well as electric vehicles.
